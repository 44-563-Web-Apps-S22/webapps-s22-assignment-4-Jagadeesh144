The Miser

ACT III
scene III
The Coversation between Damis and Dorine and Laurent

The Miser

[observing Dorine].

Laurent, lock up my hair shirt and my scourge, And pray for freedom from each carnal urge. If anyone comes calling, say I have gone To share my alms with the poor souls in prison.

HARPAGON

Such affectation and boastful behavior! Tartuffe. What do you wish?

Tartuffe

Wait! By our Savior, Please! Before you speak take this handkerchief.

LA FLÈCHE.
